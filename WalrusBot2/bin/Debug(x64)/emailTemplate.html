Go ahead and get our free membership on SUSU's website to truly start engaging with the society! Once you're a member, you'll be able to come along and play at events and join our growing community of passionate gamers!
With this free membership, you can become a member of the University of Southampton Video Games and eSports Society. As well as joining our Discord server, you'll find help with joining teams for competitive gaming, can access our community game servers, and will be able to play on our games consoles and VR equipment during special events run by the society!
In addition to the perks of community membership, DLC Bundle members will also be able to experience VR and use consoles at our game nights. We currently have a wireless HTC Vive VR headset, a Nintendo Switch, and an XBox One. We will also run regular bring-your-own-games-and-consoles nights, which will be free for everyone!
In addition to the perks of community membership, Game-of-the-Year Edition membership entitles the user to come along and play Steam games on University PCs when we run game nights.
